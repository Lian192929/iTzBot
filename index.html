Our Frequently asked questions

What is iTzBot?

iTzBot stands as the ultimate Discord bot, with an elegant and user-friendly interface. Featuring buttons, modals, embeds and customization, and a plethora of highly effective features, iTzBot reigns as the premier bot in the market, offering Discord users an unparalleled way to enhance their Discord journey.

Is iTzBot free?

Absolutely, iTzBot is and will continue to be completely free. This commitment to free access remains steadfast as long as the developers are able to sustain this model.

How do I use iTzBot?

Easy! Just type / in the chat to open up the slash command panel, then enter 'help' and select the iTzBot command. From there, you can engage with the help menu and access all the information you require

How do I add iTzBot to my Discord server?

You can add iTzBot to your Discord server by clicking the "Add to Discord" button on our website or by using the provided invite link. Once added, you can customize its features and settings.

Where can I find more information about iTzBot's commands?

You can find detailed information about iTzBot's commands by using the slash command "/help" in your server's chat. This will provide you with a comprehensive list of available commands and their descriptions.

Can I contribute to iTzBot's development?

Currently, our development team handles Airwavy's development. However, we appreciate your interest! You can support us by providing feedback, reporting issues, and spreading the word about Airwavy.

Can I customize iTzBot's settings?

Absolutely! iTzBot offers a wide range of customization options to tailor its functionality to your server's requirements. You can easily configure its features and behaviors using both commands and the user-friendly dashboard. This allows you to create a unique experience for your community without any hassle.
